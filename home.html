Our Mission Statement

At Myra Beauty Spa, our mission is to empower young women living in townships by providing them with opportunities to grow and succeed in the beauty industry. Located near Busy Corner Shisanyama in Ebony Park, we hire young ladies, offer them free training, and prepare them for positions as professional beauty therapists.

Our goal extends beyond employment — we aim to nurture future entrepreneurs by equipping them with the skills and confidence to eventually establish their own beauty clinics. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service, offering comprehensive training, and instilling strong values in our team to ensure their personal and professional success.
